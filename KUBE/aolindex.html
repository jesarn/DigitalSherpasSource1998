AOL Windows Users:
AOL HAS THREE DIFFERENT 3.0 VERSIONS... YOU MUST HAVE THE LATEST (WINDOWS 95) VERSION. TO CHECK WHICH VERSION OF AOL YOU HAVE INSTALLED GO TO "HELP" AND "ABOUT AOL" IN THE TOP TASK BAR. IF IT READS "AOL 3.0 FOR WINDOWS" YOU DON'T HAVE THE CURRENT VERSION. IT NEEDS TO SAY AOL 3.O FOR WINDOWS 95. IF YOU HAVE AOL 3.0 FOR WINDOWS AND DO NOT WANT TO UPGRADE, YOU NEED TO DOWNLOAD THE 16bit (windows 3.1) VERSION OF FLASH. TO DO THIS CLICK HERE.

  1. Log onto your AOL account as normal... make sure you have the latest version of AOL software, including the Internet Explorer integrated browser. Once you have installed the most recent version of AOL software, you may click on the "ENTER HERE" button on this page or at http://www.kube93.com to enter the site.
  2. If you are unsure what version of AOL software you have, or you need assistance in downloading the latest AOL software, go to keyword: TECH LIVE for assistance.


AOL Macintosh Users:

The Macintosh verion of AOL software does not yet support JavaScript, which is necessary to navigate through Pepsi World. To view Pepsi World with a JavaScript-capable browser, your browser should either be
Netscape Navigator 3.0 [or higher full release version]
or
Microsoft Internet Explorer 3.0 [or higher full release version]

You may use Netscape or Internet Explorer in tandem with your AOL connection. If you need technical assistance downloading browsers or updating to the most current version of AOL software, go to
AOL keyword: TECH LIVE for step by step instructions.
