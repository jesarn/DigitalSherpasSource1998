St. Patrick's Day Dash

Join us for the 14th Annual Q-13 St. Patrick's Day Dash!

Sunday, March 15th at 9 a.m., walk, jog or run the nearly four mile course. The St. Patty's Day Dash begins at T. S. McHugh's Public House on Mercer Street and ends at F.X. McRory's near the Kingdome. Parking is available at the Kingdome for $5. Buses will then shuttle racers from the Kingdome to T. S. McHugh's free of charge. The last bus will leave at 8:30 a.m.

Pick your level by choosing a wave:
* Serious runners leave first in the "green wave" at 9 a.m.
* Other runners leave in the "red wave" for runners at 9:10 a.m.
* Those who plan to run/walk leave in the "yellow wave" at 9:20a.m.
* The final group - baby joggers, strollers, wagons and walkers - the "white wave," leaves at 9:30 a.m.

Cost:
* $15 - if you sign up before Feb. 22nd.
* $20 - if you sign up between Feb. 23rd and March 13th.
* $25 - if you sign up on March 14th at Super Jock 'n Jill (only late registration location).
*  There is no day-of-the-race registration.

Registration forms are available at Super Jock 'n Jill, Fast Lady Sports, Redmond Athletic Club and Sound Sports or print one online. Additional registration forms are available at Car Toys and Sprint PCS Stores.

For information call (800) 683-8393 or (425) 489-2447.
