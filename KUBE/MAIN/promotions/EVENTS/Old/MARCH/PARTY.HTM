Fabulously Swanky Grammy Party

Dress up and watch the Grammy awards in style with KUBE 93! This is your invitation to KUBE 93's Fabulously Swanky Grammy Party.

When: Wednesday, 2/25
Time: 7-11 p.m.
Where: Planet Hollywood (6th and Pike, downtown Seattle)

* There is no cover charge to attend.
* Bobby O will broadcast live from 7-11pm from Planet Hollywood.
* Dress up - swingin', swanky, cocktail lounge party style.
* All attendees will be able to predict the Grammy winners in Best Rap
Performance Duo or Group, Best R& B Album, Best Rap Solo Performance, Best R& B Song, and Best Rap Album.  If you pick all 5 correctly, your name will go into a drawing for a 50 disc CD player!

Listen to KUBE 93 to win tickets to the special KUBE 93 VIP celebrity room at Planet Hollywood. Winners will enjoy free food & beverage, and get to meet and greet with KUBE 93 & local celebrities.
