The 3rd Annual Gary Everson

Tournament

When: Saturday, April 18th, 1998
Where: Seattle Center, Mercer Arena

Playing PICTIONARY® is a blast! So, imagine playing PICTIONARY® for great prizes in the Seattle Center Mercer Arena. Sound like Fun? Well, it is. And best of all, the tournament is for a great cause - raising money for children and teens affected by AIDS. To compete in the tournament, each team needs to raise a minimum of $400 in sponsorships ($100 registration fee required when you sign up to participate). Space is limited so register today!

For more information and registration forms, please contact the Rise 'n Shine PICTIONARY® Tournament Office at (206) 628-8949
