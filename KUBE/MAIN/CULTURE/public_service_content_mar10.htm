PAWS WILDLIFE
Spring and summer are the busiest times of
the year for the PAWS Wildlife Rehabilitation
Center.  Over the next several months,
hundreds of orphaned birds and mammals
will be brought to the paws wildlife center for
intensive care.  We invite you to help us raise
these young animals so they can be released
back into the wild.  If you can spare three to
five hours a week, please call (425) 787-2500,
ext. 808

SSI CHILDRENS PROJECT
Almost one-quarter million American families with disabled children face the loss of Supplemental Security Income.  If your family has been notified that your child can no longer receive SSI, there is still time to appeal.  You can get free legal advice.  To find out how, call the Children's SSI Pro Bono Panel at 1-800-878-9383.

CATHOLIC COMM. SERVICES
Catholic Community Services wants your help.  Nearly 100 persons a month call Volunteer Chore Services asking for someone to drive a senior to the grocery store twice a month, or assist a person with a disability clean their apartment.  Would you be one of those "someones"?  If interested, call (206) 324-5247.

WOODLAND PARK ZOO
If you like to work with both animals and people, Woodland Park zoo is the place for you.  Sign up to volunteer this spring and summer at one of the zoo's most popular exhibits, the Family Farm Contact Area.  You must be at least 15, be available  to work a regular four-hour shift and successfully complete a training program.  For more information call (206) 684-4021.
