KUBE 93 is proud to be part of your community. Many events
and charitable organizations in the Puget Sound area
receive support from KUBE 93. Public Service
Announcements (PSAs) are one way KUBE 93
provides its audience with information about particular
events or organizations which support our community.

To submit information to KUBE 93 for a PSA,
here's what you need to do:

* Fax an informational sheet to: KUBE 93 FM,
Attn: Tony B., (206) 286-2376; or
* Mail an informational packet to: KUBE 93 FM,
Attn: Tony B., 190 Queen Anne Avenue North,
Suite 100, Seattle, WA 98109.

Please be sure to include details on your
event/organization, relevant dates/times,
location, how you'd like KUBE 93 to be
involved and a 25-second script of how you'd
like your PSA to read. After you submit your
event/organization's information to KUBE 93,
please follow up in about a week with a phone
call to Tony B. (206-285-2295, ext. 702)
during KUBE 93 business hours (8:30am -
5:30mp, M-F).

Please note - KUBE 93 reserves the right to modify the PSA script prior to airing and produce the script based on station guidelines. PSAs must be approved by KUBE 93 FM and will air according to station guidelines.
