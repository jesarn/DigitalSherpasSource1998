career summary:
I BEGAN MY CAREER IN HIGH SCHOOL, DJ’ING AT BELLEVUE H.S. AND PROCEEDED TO WORK AT SEVERAL STATIONS WHILE AT WSU, INCLUDING 2-FUN106. I ALSO HAD THE DISTINCT PLEASURE OF BEING AN ANNOUNCER ON AN AWARD-WINNING GAME SHOW IN COLLEGE AND SERVED AS THE PUBLIC ADDRESS ANNOUNCER FOR THE WSU VOLLEYBALL TEAM.

BY FAR, BEING HIRED AT KUBE 93 HAS BEEN MY TURNING POINT AND FULFILLMENT OF A DREAM!!! DID A TEAR JUST ROLL DOWN YOUR CHEEK?

other jobs you've had along the way:
I GOT MY ROCKS OFF AS A BOWLING ALLEY ATTENDANT, AND THREW A FEW NEWSPAPERS ON TO THE DOOR MATS OF INQUIRING MINDS THAT WANTED TO KNOW SOMETHING.

career mentor/hero:
FORMER KUBE 93 PERSONALITY GARY HART WAS THE FIRST GOOF BALL KIND ENOUGH TO LET ME SIT IN ON HIS SHOW PLUS HIS USE OF "THE SOUND EFFECT" IMPACTED ME IN A STRANGE WAY (I WOULD FIND MYSELF IMITATING HIS SOUND EFFECTS IN THE SHOWER THE NEXT DAY).

reason listeners love your show:
NOBODY ADMITS IT BUT EVERYONE WANTS TO BE CLOSE TO SOMEONE WHO IS A LITTLE STRANGE AND CLOSE TO INSANITY…CUDDLE UP TO YOUR BOOMBOX AND ENJOY THE RIDE.

con's
I’VE BEEN DISAPPOINTED BY SEVERAL ARTISTS’ LACK OF A SOLID SOPHOMORE ALBUM. THE DON’T NECESSARILY HAVE TO REPEAT THEIR FIRST ALBUM BUT GIVE REINVENTION A SHOT.
