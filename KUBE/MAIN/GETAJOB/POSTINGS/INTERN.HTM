KUBE 93 FM PROMOTIONS INTERN

KUBE 93 is currently seeking interns for the promotions department for the Spring and/or Summer 1998.  Interns for KUBE 93 are considered a hire of New Century Media and must represent the company and specifically our station in a positive and professional manner. Promotion interns enrolled in school and attending class may receive credit toward graduation through completion of the internship.

This is an unpaid position. The intern may be evaluated at the end of the term for a potential part time promotions position, depending on staffing needs at the time. The promotions intern reports directly to the KUBE 93 Promotions Director and the KUBE 93 Promotions Coordinator.

KUBE 93 Promotions Intern Duties:
* Assist in executing station events, 93 minute sticker stops, movie premieres, station promoted concerts, and sales remotes and appearances.
* Help with office work including: organizing and maintenance of KUBE 93 prizes and prize winners, faxing, filing, and executing mailings.
*  Act as liaison between KUBE 93 and the listening public.

KUBE 93 Promotions Intern Qualifications:
* Outgoing personality
* Responsible
* Energetic
* Team player
* Detail oriented
* Interested in radio promotions/marketing
