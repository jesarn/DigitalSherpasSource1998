Time To Bail: Newsweek Magazine has an interesting story on hip-hop's number one bail/bondsman; and his name is Josh Herman. In the magazine, Herman speaks of how his fame erupted after having Snoop Doggy Dogg as his client. According to the article, Herman has a family history of dealing with celebrities and the law.

Slamming Cube: We've just heard that the westside's killer mc Ice Cube is getting into professional wrestling. According to our rumor mill, a wrestling agent approached Cube with the idea. The rumor goes on to say that since Ice Cube's a gangster turned righteous leader turned gangster turned good guy, he's perfect. It's also rumored that Cube will have a special move called the Two-Faced Tomahawk.
