Shelter Cove


DOZENS OF HOMELESS PEOPLE WITH PSYCHIATRIC DISORDERS HAVE FOUND A SAFE PLACE TO LIVE ON THE FORMER FORT ORD ARMY BASE.

THIS MORNING. RESIDENTS OF "SHELTER-COVE". LOCAL POLITICIANS AND OTHER SUPPORTERS GATHERED TO CELEBRATE THE GRAND OPENING OF THE NEW COMMUNITY ROOM.

SHELTER COVE IS A 37 BED TRANSITIONAL HOUSING COMPLEX.

THIS IS ONE MORE STEP FORWARD IN BATTLE AGAINST STIGMA. THE MENTALLY ILL ARE MISPERCIEVED. EVERY TIME WE OPEN A DOOR LIKE THIS. COMMUNITY CAN MEET THESE PEOPLE. STEP FORWARD IN LONG STRUGGLE.

THIS IS THE FIRST "NEW" STRUCTURE BUILT ON THE OLD ARMY BASE SINCE FORT ORD CLOSED.

THE CITY'S BUILDING INSPECTOR POSTED A STOP-WORK ORDER AT THE OLD STOHAN'S ART GALLERY THIS AFTERNOON.

Teen Drinking


NEW INFORMATION TONIGHT ON THE ROOTS OF ALCOHOLISM.

A NEW GOVERNMENT STUDY FINDS A STRONG LINK BETWEEN TEEN DRINKING AND ALCOHOLISM LATER IN LIFE.

IF YOUR CHILD STARTS DRINKING AT OR BEFORE THE AGE OF FIFTEEN.HE OR SHE HAS A FORTY PERCENT CHANCE OF BECOMING AN ALCOHOLIC AS AN ADULT.

THAT NUMBER IS NEARLY CUT IN HALF. IF A YOUNG PERSON BEGINS DRINKING AT SEVENTEEN.

IT GOES DOWN TO TEN PERCENT IF DRINKING IS PUT OFF UNTIL AGE TWENTY-ONE.

N.E. Weather


CREWS STILL REPAIRING THE SHATTERED ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS IN PARTS OF EASTERN CANADA AND THE U-S. AFTER LAST WEEK'S ICE STORMS.

AN ESTIMATED 400-THOUSAND PEOPLE ARE STILL WITHOUT POWER. AND A STATE OF EMERGENCY REMAINS IN EFFECT. WITH TEMPERATURES DROPPING TO ZERO. OR BELOW.

HEALTH OFFICIALS SAY.THE ICE STORM HAS CREATED AN EPIDEMIC OF CARBON MONOXIDE POISONINGS.

PEOPLE SICKENED BY EXPOSURE TO THE GAS HAVE FLOODED EMERGENCY ROOMS.

RESIDENTS WITHOUT POWER BEGAN USING GENERATORS AND KEROSENE HEATERS TO STAY WARM.
