"I Hear a Symphony" - December 10

Olivia Newton-John Guest Stars as Herself

Murphy's bout with cancer has her thinking a lot about the things she'd like to do - climb Mount Everest, memorize all the words to American Pie, and learn how to juggle. When Corky tells her that a charity auction is offering the chance to conduct a symphony orchestra, Murphy adds "maestro for an evening" to the list. But, once at the auction, she receives heavy one-on-one competition from overzealous bidder Olivia Newton-John.
