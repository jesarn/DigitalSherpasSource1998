Au Pair


BRITISH NANNY LOUISE WOODWARD COULD LEARN HER FATE TODAY.

A RULING MAY COME DOWN THIS MORNING ON WHETHER THE SECOND DEGREE MURDER CONVICTION FOR WOODWARD WILL STAND.

THE JUDGE IN THE CASE IS EXPECTED TO ISSUE HIS DECISION OVER THE INTERNET.

LOCAL PUBS IN WOODWARD'S HOMETOWN OF ELTON ENGLAND HAVE ALREADY SET UP COMPUTER FACILITIES SO THEY CAN INSTANTLY ACCESS THE RULING.

19-YEAR-OLD WOODWARD WAS CONVICTED OF MURDERING AN INFANT LEFT IN HER CARE.

THE JUDGE COULD UPHOLD THE CONVICTION, REDUCE IT TO MANSLAUGHTER, ORDER A NEW TRIAL. OR ACQUIT WOODWARD.

Hillary/Air Force One


A CLOSE CALL FOR FIRST LADY HILLARY CLINTON, ABOARD A PLANE FORMERLY USED AS AIR FORCE ONE.

THE FIRST LADY WAS ON HER WAY FROM WASHINGTON TO RUSSIA LAST NIGHT, AS PART OF A 5-NATION TOUR, WHEN HER BOEING 707 EXPERIENCED ENGINE PROBLEMS, JUST 10-MINUTES AFTER TAKEOFF.

THE PLANE DUMPED FUEL AND RETURNED TO ANDREWS AIR FORCE BASE, WHERE IT WAS GREETED BY A HALF DOZEN FIRE TRUCKS.

MAINTENANCE WORKERS FOUND A FRAYED WIRE IN AN OUTBOARD ENGINE ON THE LEFT WING.

Princess Diana


AN EGYPTIAN LAWYER IS SUING QUEEN ELIZABETH AND THE SECRET SERVICE, BECAUSE HE BELIEVES THEY ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR THE DEATH OF PRINCESS DIANA.

THE CAIRO MAN CLAIMS THE DEATHS OF DIANA AND DODI FAYED WERE PLANNED BY INTELLIGENCE AGENTS TO PREVENT THEM FROM MARRYING, AND EVENTUALLY HAVING A MUSLIM CHILD.

THE SUIT ALLEGES THE QUEEN DIDN'T WANT ENGLAND'S FUTURE KING TO HAVE MUSLIM SIBLINGS.

THE VALIDITY OF THE 150-THOUSAND DOLLAR SUIT WILL BE CONSIDERED BY A CAIRO COURT NEXT MONTH.

Fast Track


IN WASHINGTON, A CONTROVERSIAL VOTE ON THE FAST TRACK TRADE LEGISLATION HAS BEEN PUT ON HOLD THIS MORNING.

THE LEGISLATION WOULD GIVE THE PRESIDENT THE POWER TO NEGOTIATE TRADE AGREEMENTS.

CONGRESS WOULD BE ALLOWED TO APPROV OR REJECT THE

AGREEMENTS.BUT NOT CHANGE THEM.

AFTER A WEEKEND OF INTENSE LOBBYING, THE PRESIDENT AND G-O-P LEADERS AGREED TO DELAY A VOTE ON THE MEASURE.

DEMOCRATIC LAWMAKERS SAY THERE ISN'T ENOUGH SUPPORT TO ASSURE PASSAGE.

THE PRESIDENT AND HOUSE SPEAKER NEWT GINGRICH WILL TALK TODAY TO AND DECIDE ON THE NEXT STEP.

Triple Shoot Folo


MEANWHILE, SALINAS POLICE AREN'T ACTIVELY LOOKING FOR SUSPECTS IN SATURDAY'S DOUBLE SHOOTING ON MOHAR STREET.

THEY BELIEVE THE MAN RESPONSIBLE FOR THE ATTACK ON HIS EX-GIRL FRIEND AND HER CURRENT BOYFRIEND. KILLED HIMSELF HOURS LATER.

INVESTIGATORS FOUND THE BODY MATCHING THE DESCRIPTION OF 25-YEAR OLD ELIZAR MENDEZ, ON OLD STAGE ROAD SATURDAY AFTERNOON.

AN AUTOPSY WILL BE CONDUCTED THIS MORNING TO POSTIVELY IDENTIFY THE BODY.

THE CASE IS BEING TREATED AS A DOMESTIC VIOLENCE INCIDENT.

THE VICTIMS ARE CURRENTLY IN STABLE CONDITION AT NATIVIDAD MEDICAL CENTER.

Car Seats Donated


CAR SEATS HAVE PROVEN TO BE LIFE SAVERS. BUT FOR MANY FAMILIES IT'S A LUXURY TO OWN ONE.

SO THE MONTEREY COUNTY SAFE KIDS COALITION HAS ORGANIZED A CAR SEAT GIVE AWAY.

CAR SEATS WILL BE GIVEN OUT TO SEVERAL NEEDY FAMILIES THIS AFTERNOON THAT HAVE BEEN CHOSEN FOR THE GIVE AWAY BASED ON THEIR ECONOMIC HARDSHIPS.

THE SEATS WILL BE GIVEN AWAY AT A CERMONY AT THE PERINATAL RECOVERY SERVICES IN SALINAS THIS AFTERNOON.

THE CAR SEATS WERE DONATED BY THE GENERAL MOTORS CORPORATION.

Visit the National Safe Kids Coalition Site to learn more about the organization.
