Endotech™ Cuff
Our patented Endotech Cuff is a hinged support cuff hidden inside the walls of the SoftBoot. Designed for performance with top notch comfort and support, it gives advanced skaters more agility and freedom of movement, with just the right amount of stability for maximum power. 		 
 
  Exotech™ Cuff
Our Exotech Cuff is a hinged support cuff located outside the SoftBoot™ for exceptional lateral stability and plenty of forward flex. Built just like your ankle, it's perfect for skaters of any ability level who want a light, comfortable, high performance skate.
