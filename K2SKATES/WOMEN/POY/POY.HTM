Women's Flight

 Women's feet, calves and ankles have a special physiology all their own. That's why we build our women's skates on a custom-designed women's last, in half sizes. It's also why we've done extensive research on men's and women's foot structures and biomechanics. And why even our cuff designs are specially suited to men's and women's specific needs. In fact, we're the only skate maker in the world to design true men's- and women's-specific skates. Yet another reason K2's are the best fitting, most comfortable skates you can buy. Whether your hormone of choice is estrogen or testosterone.
