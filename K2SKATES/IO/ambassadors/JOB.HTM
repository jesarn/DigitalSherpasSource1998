Field Marketing / Consumer Sales, K2 Inline Skates



Market areas: Dallas Texas, Chicago area, Denver area.

America's leading manufacturer of high performance soft boot inline skates, has immediate part time openings for mature, highly responsible individuals in our field marketing team. The K2 inline field marketing team (Ambassadors) represents the K2 brand in high profile retail stores and regional promotions. This positions entails leading and motivating retail staff by demonstrating exemplary sales and customer service skills on the retail floor and continuously reinforcing K2 product knowledge. Regional Ambassadors will be responsible for sales, merchandising, motivating retail sales associates, gathering of market intelligence, and assisting with other K2 promotional events. This entry level positions offers comprehensive training, supervised by the Regional Ambassador program coordinator and will work closely with the local sales rep, National Ambassador team, and retail mangers and staff. All candidates will have 5 days of training & orientation at K2 headquarters (Seattle) starting 4/29/98. Positions start in markets on 5/08/98.

College aged students, recent graduates, and others with additional non-weekend part time employment encouraged to apply.

This is not a sales rep position.

Minimum qualifications:
  • Superior retail sales skills with 2 + years retail sales experience. Retail management a plus.
  • Professional appearance and conduct.
  • Basic instruction or leadership experience.
  • Active sports oriented lifestyle.
  • Interest in Inline Skating.
  • Must have their own vehicle.
  • Flexibility to travel up to 25 miles to various local retails within their local market area.
  • IISA instructor certification a plus.
