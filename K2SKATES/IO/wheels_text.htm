K2 Double Density: a better wheel

Hard wheels for a fast ride. Soft wheels for smooth one. Those are the rules, right? Well, screw the rules. By combining two types of urethane in a single wheel, our new patented Double Density wheels give you a soft wheel ride with hard wheel speed and durability. So. Any other rules you'd like to break?

Double Density wheels available on Power X 5.0, VO2 Max, Escape and Freedom. Look for separately sold 4-packs available before the end of this year.

The Hub
Unlike conventional nylon hubs, this one's made of urethane and it's chemically bonded to the wheel. That makes it snappier, smoother, and better able to absorb road vibrations.

The Inner Core
Made of a softer, higher-rebound urethane, the inner layer acts like a shock absorber, smoothing out your ride and providing better grip at high speeds. Durometer: 70A-72A.

The Outer Tire
Harder, more durable urethane makes the outer wheel blindingly fast and erosion-resistant, but thanks to the soft inner core you don't sacrifice smoothness or comfort. Durometer: 78A-80A.
