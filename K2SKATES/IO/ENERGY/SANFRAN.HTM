San Francisco

The Friday Night Skate in San Francisco begins in front of the clock tower on Embarcadero Street at 9:00 p.m. sharp. David Miles, the skate director, has been running the event for 10 years through the city streets in San Francisco. Approximately 100-300 people skate side by side for 12 miles right on the waterfront and throughout downtown, stopping in five different locations to pick up any stragglers. Safety comes first! The National Skate Patrol accompanies skaters and traffic throughout the Skate, stopping downtown traffic for a mere 300 skaters during the entire event. Anyone and everyone is welcome to join in on the fun!
