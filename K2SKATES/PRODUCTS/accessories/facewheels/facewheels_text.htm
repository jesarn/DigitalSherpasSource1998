Face Wheels are gravity poured. Does this take way longer? Yes. Does it cost us more? Yes. Does it provide a superior core-to-wheel bond and eliminate delams, bumps and air bubbles? Yes, yes, and yes. Did we stop there? No. We also equipped them with urethane cores instead of conventional nylon ones. Why? Because it gives 'em a snappier, damper feel instead of a stiffer, deader one. And 80 years from now is soon enough to be stiff and dead.
